Overview

Komx Reader is an offline-first manga and comic reader. It primarily processes image data for reading and enhancement (AI Colorization) and allows you to browse web content via an integrated browser. Most processing occurs locally on your device.

Core Privacy Principles:
  • Offline-First: Your library, history, and categories are stored locally in a private database on your device.
  • Local AI Processing: Features like AI Colorization and OCR (Optical Character Recognition) are processed entirely on your device. Your images never leave your phone.
  • Browser Privacy: Our integrated browser is for your convenience. We do not track, record, or store your browsing history.
  • File Access: The app only accesses files you explicitly navigate to in the File Explorer or add to your library.
  • No Account Required: You can use all core features without providing an email address or creating an account.
  • Transparency: Background tasks like AI processing are always visible via system notifications.

Information We Collect

Local Data (Stored on Your Device)

  • Manga library metadata (Titles, URLs, Thumbnails)
  • Reading progress and history
  • Custom tags and categories
  • App settings and preferences
  • Temporary Cache: Extracted image URLs from the browser to facilitate the reading interface.

Third-Party Data Collection

  • Google Play Services: Device information, account authentication for subscriptions, and billing data.
  • Google Play Billing: Purchase transactions and subscription status tokens. We do not see or store your credit card details.
  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics: Anonymized usage statistics and crash reports to help us improve app stability.

Subscriptions & Premium Features

We offer Komx Reader PRO subscriptions processed through Google Play Billing:

Purchase Information:

  • Transaction details (date, product ID) are managed by Google.
  • We receive a non-identifiable token to verify your PRO status.
  • Premium Privacy: Premium users are not subject to any advertising data collection (if advertisements are integrated in the future).

Permissions & Data Usage

  • Storage Access (All Files): Required to find and display your local comic archives (CBZ, CBR, etc.).
  • Internet: Required for the integrated Web Browser and to verify subscription status with Google.
  • Notifications: Used to show progress for long-running AI tasks.
  • Foreground Services: Used to ensure AI Colorization tasks are not interrupted if you switch apps.

Browser Translate & Image Extraction

When using the "Browse" feature:

  • The app captures visual elements of the web view solely to provide a reader interface. This is processed locally.
  • We do not monitor or store passwords or personal data entered into the web browser.
  • Cookies and site data are managed by the system WebView and can be cleared via your device settings.

Security

We prioritize your data security. By processing your manga and AI tasks locally, your most sensitive data (your images) never leaves your control. Payment processing uses Google's secure infrastructure. While we implement best practices, please remember that no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.
