Privacy Policy for Komx Reader

Last updated: May 20, 2024

At Komx Reader (also known as Haku Reader), accessible as a mobile application, the privacy of our users is of extreme importance. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect and how we use it.

Summary: Komx Reader is designed to be a private, offline-first application. We do not own servers that store your manga, reading history, or personal files.

1. Data Collection & Local Storage

Komx Reader operates primarily as a local tool for viewing your own content. Here is how your data is handled:

  • Library & History: All information regarding your manga library, reading progress, categories, and custom tags is stored locally on your device in a private database.
  • Local Files: When you use the File Explorer to read local archives (CBZ, CBR, PDF, etc.), the app only accesses these files to display them to you. No file content is uploaded to any external server.
  • AI Features: AI Colorization and OCR (Optical Character Recognition) are processed locally on your device or via temporary system resources. No images from your library are permanently stored or shared by us during these processes.

2. Web Browser Features

If you use the integrated Web Browser to read manga online:

  • The app functions as a standard web browser. Website owners may collect data as they would in any other browser (cookies, IP addresses).
  • Komx Reader may temporarily extract image URLs to provide a cleaner reading experience. This data is transient and not shared with third parties.

3. Subscriptions & Billing

If you purchase a Komx Reader PRO subscription:

  • All transactions are handled securely by the Google Play Store.
  • We do not collect or store your credit card information, billing address, or real name.
  • We receive a non-identifiable purchase token from Google to verify your subscription status and unlock premium features.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use standard mobile analytics (such as Firebase Analytics) to understand app stability and feature usage. This data is:

  • Anonymous: We cannot identify you personally through this data.
  • Aggregated: It helps us see which features are popular and identify crashes to improve the app.

5. Permissions

The app requires specific permissions to function:

  • Storage Access: Required to read your local manga files and save colorized results.
  • Internet: Required for the Web Browser feature and to verify subscription status.

6. Children's Privacy

Komx Reader does not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13. If you believe your child has provided such information, please contact us, and we will remove it immediately.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this page periodically.
